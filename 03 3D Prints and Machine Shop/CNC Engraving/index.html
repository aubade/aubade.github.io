03 3D Prints and Machine Shop » CNC Engraving

These were both produced on a Haas CNC Mill. The small keychain was produced with MasterCam X6, while the larger plate was created from hand-written G-Code based on an original design.

The engraving on the keychain reads "Ειναι για μενα Ελληνικα", which means "It's Greek to me" in Greek.
